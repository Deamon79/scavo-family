I. A note before the showings

Dear Owner,

A short note before we meet. We are Damian and Karolina, with Gabriel (10) and Michael (5). We’ve been in the Bay Area since 2013 — the last three of those years on Santa Margarita Avenue, in Menlo Park, and the three before that on Berkeley Avenue, a few blocks away. Our current landlord has decided to sell, which is the only reason we are looking. We would have stayed indefinitely.

We are not ready to buy until next year, when we plan to settle for the long term. So we are looking for a one-year home (with the option to extend), where we can take care of your house as if it were our own. That part is not a slogan: a home is the place where my children grow up.

The lease will be in my name personally, but the rent is paid directly by Streetbeat — the AI fintech company I founded and run as CEO. Streetbeat closed a $15M Series A in October 2025 (lead investor: CDP Venture Capital, Italy’s sovereign-wealth-backed fund), and the housing benefit is part of my compensation package. As an additional layer, our personal guarantor is Maciej Donajski, Streetbeat’s CTO and co-founder. The rent is, frankly, one of the most predictable line-items on our books.

If you are reading this, you have probably already met other applicants. Below you’ll find everything you might want to know — including phone numbers for both of our previous landlords, who will pick up.

Thank you for considering us.

IV. Why we’re moving

Three quiet years.
And one decision that wasn’t ours.

We’ve had three calm and happy years on Santa Margarita Avenue. Our landlord, whom we genuinely adored, decided this spring to sell the house. We would have stayed.

We are not in a position to buy yet — we want to take a year, find the neighborhood that feels permanent for the boys, and only then put roots down. So this is a one-year-with-extension lease, with us treating your home with the same care we gave the previous two.

We are looking for: 3+ bedrooms, a yard if possible, walking or short driving distance to a good elementary school, and a kitchen Karolina can cook in. We sign for a year starting whenever the property is ready; we are flexible on furnished or unfurnished.
